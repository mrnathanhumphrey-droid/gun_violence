## Sundown towns data build

- **Source:** Tougaloo / Loewen Sundown Towns Database, scraped 2026-05-15 from `https://justice.tougaloo.edu/location/<statename>/` for all 50 states (1 sec/page throttle, ~50 sec total wall).
- **2,479 raw entries** (2,437 suspected sundown + 42 starred non-sundown). Starred entries excluded.
- **City → county mapping:** Census Places 2024 gazetteer (`2024_Gaz_place_national.txt`, 32,333 places, 1.2 MB) → spatial join centroid lat/lon → TIGER 2024 county polygon in EPSG:5070 equal-area.
- **Match rate:** 1,913 of 2,437 (78%). Unmatched 22% are mostly "X County" entries (county-level listings) and a few misspelled / alternate names; defensible to drop for the v0.4 county-level merge.
- **Coverage:** 950 of 3,235 US counties have at least one documented sundown town.
- **Top counties:** DuPage IL (29), Cook IL (27), LA CA (23), Cuyahoga OH (22), Milwaukee WI (15) — large metros with historically restrictive racial covenants.
- **Joint coverage:** 140 counties have both HOLC + sundown, 17 counties have all three (HOLC + VRA + sundown).
- **Per-cell distribution:** sundown concentrates in urban + white-rural cells (UW-LI 80/135, UB-HI 41/71, UH-HI 39/64, RW-HI 16/46, SB-MC 16/67). Rural minority cells get few hits (RB-HI 1, RH-HI 7, RNA-HI 1).

Build script: [analysis/build_v04_sundown.py](D:/Gun Violence/analysis/build_v04_sundown.py). Output: [analysis/historical_features_county_v04.parquet](D:/Gun Violence/analysis/historical_features_county_v04.parquet).

## Plain-English headline

**In US counties at this resolution, two distinct historical mechanisms — New Deal-era redlining (built-environment / property-finance) AND mid-20th-century sundown towns (explicit racial exclusion / social terrorism) — each independently predict present-day firearm-death rates after controlling for current race composition, structural inequity, geography, and SES.** Voting-rights preclearance coverage (VRA Section 4(b)) is null. The race × inequity entanglement from v0.2 replicates intact through v0.4.

The methodology corpus's contribution here: structural priors over residual classes + explicit historical-mechanism markers surfaced THREE CI-clean structural findings (race × inequity interaction, HOLC redlining, sundown towns) that an additive-baseline model with current race + SES + geo alone would have missed.
