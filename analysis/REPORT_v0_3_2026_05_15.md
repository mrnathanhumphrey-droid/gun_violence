## TL;DR

- **HOLC redlining share-D** is a **CI-clean positive predictor** of county firearm-death rate after controlling for cell, race composition, inequity composite, geo-type, and 6 SES covariates: **β = +0.682 [+0.324, +1.049]**.

## Historical mechanism coefficients

| Term | Mean | 95% CI | CI clean? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **HOLC share-D** | **+0.682** | **[+0.324, +1.049]** | **YES** |
| HOLC any (binary) | −0.109 | [−0.263, +0.043] | no |
| VRA Section 4(b) | +0.055 | [−0.059, +0.167] | no |

**Interpretation:**

- HOLC share-D's coefficient = +0.68 on the standardized scale means going from a county with 0% redlining (share-D = 0) to one with 100% D-graded HOLC area (share-D = 1) predicts ~exp(0.68) ≈ **1.98× higher firearm-death rate** — after controlling for cell, race, inequity, geo, and SES. This is a substantial conditional effect, and the CI excludes zero with margin to spare.

- HOLC any (binary) being null *after* share-D is in the model means: among HOLC-mapped counties, what matters is *how much* of the mapped area was D-graded, not the mere fact that the county was mapped. Both terms in the model together: the "any HOLC at all" effect is absorbed by the continuous share-D.

- VRA Section 4(b) being null is informative. Pre-Shelby preclearance coverage — a marker that *should* track structural-discrimination intensity — adds **no independent predictive power** for firearm-death rate after race + inequity + HOLC are in the model. Two readings:
  1. VRA coverage was a *voting-rights* marker, not a *physical-built-environment* marker. HOLC is built-environment + property-finance; VRA is voting-access. The mechanisms of action for firearm-death rate may operate through built-environment channels more than through voting-access channels.
  2. Pre-Shelby VRA coverage is also strongly correlated with cell (Southern cells absorb most VRA counties), so the cell intercept may be eating the VRA signal.

|---|---|---|
| H_INEQUITY (pure) | eta_inequity share still small (~9% of additive); only RH-HI cell has CI-clean inequity slope and it's negative | **Falsified (consistent with v0.2)** |

| H_GEOGRAPHIC_MECHANISM | geo terms still CI-span-zero; cell baseline absorbs geo structure | Inconclusive |
| **H_HISTORICAL_MECHANISM** | **HOLC share-D +0.682 [+0.324, +1.049] CI clean; VRA null** | **PARTIALLY SUPPORTED** |

**H_HISTORICAL_MECHANISM partial-support reading:** the pre-reg specified historical mechanism as a *multi-marker* disposition (HOLC + sundown + VRA together). With one of three markers (HOLC) testable and CI-clean, and another (VRA) testable and null, the disposition can't be summarily validated or falsified. The cleanest framing: **redlining (HOLC D-share) is an independent contributor to county firearm-death rate that survives all available controls; voting-rights preclearance coverage is not.** The remaining sundown marker is needed before the disposition gets a final verdict.
